Hi, I'm Mallaiah

Medical Coding Aspirant

A Medical Coder plays a vital role in the healthcare industry by converting medical diagnoses, procedures, and services into standardized medical codes. These codes are essential for accurate billing, insurance claims, and maintaining patient health records.

As a medical coder, I ensure accuracy, compliance, and confidentiality while working with medical documentation. I carefully review clinical notes, laboratory results, and discharge summaries to assign appropriate ICD-10-CM, CPT, and HCPCS codes.

  • CPT & HCPCS Coding
  • Medical Terminology & Anatomy
  • Disease & Drug Knowledge

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Ensure compliance
  • Maintain confidentiality
